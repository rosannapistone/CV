Profile

I got a bachelor's degree in media and communication science with focus on strategic communication. With that knowledge and experience in my pocket, I moved on to a - for the topic - relevant further education; right now I'm studying to be a Front End Developer. I respect the power of all communications, everything from texts to grimaces. I'm curious about the possibilities and challenges of digitalization - the society and its functions are going through a change digitally. How should we succeed in including everyone? Therefore, I have a great interest in UX and graphic design. To make people actually adopt and use new products - the products needs to be user-friendly, otherwise people will be excluded.

I have a great work ethic and a willingness to learn and share knowledge with any team. I value a strong corporate culture highly as I know common values, strategies and core values ​​are followed by a pleasant working climate in general and profitability in particular. The goal is that everyone in the workplace should not only know what they're doing but why they're doing it.
